Background

Daebak Chickeng is a vibrant food and beverage company specializing in Korean-style fried chicken, reflecting our commitment to authentic Korean cuisine and local roots through our playful name. Originating from Terengganu, we have established a strong presence with outlets in KTCC, Dungun, and Kemaman.

Our diverse menu features popular dishes like Boneless, Daebak Rameng, Daebak Wings, Daebak Rice, and the new Beef Bulgogi Rice Bowl, all crafted with care to ensure a unique dining experience. Signature sauces such as Signature Red, Extra Spicy, Creamy Onion, and Snow Cheese enhance our famous fried chicken.

Complementary beverages round out the meals. Dedicated to high-quality ingredients and rigorous food preparation standards, Daebak Chickeng aims to deliver an authentic Korean dining experience with a modern twist. Our mission is to create joyful dining moments, bringing people together through shared meals and delicious flavors.
